FAQs

Are Warranties included with all appliances supplied?

Only if the manufacturer offers this with the product provided.

How long do installations roughly take?

Depending on what kind of installation and size but roughly between 2-4 weeks.

Can I meet the installer before the work takes place?Yes the installer can come to your home to quote for the works

Yes the installer can come to your home to quote for the works.

Who do i contact if there is a problem on the site?

Contact Keith Knight if there is any problems on site so they can be rectified as soon as possible.

If I change my mind about a component can I change it over before it is installed?

Yes for final parts like handles or door fronts but key items like units cause delays on the jobs finishing date.

Do I have to pay for replacement parts if something gets broken?

Depending on how the item is damaged, if it is damaged by the installer we will cover the replacement, however another circumstance would have to be covered by the client.
